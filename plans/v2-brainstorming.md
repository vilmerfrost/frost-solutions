# Frost Solutions V2.0 - "Steal and Ship" Brainstorming

This document collects ideas for the V2.0 launch, focusing on consolidating features from competitors to create the ultimate "All-in-One" Construction Admin Platform.

## 1. Document Management (The "iBinder" Killer)
*Goal: Replace the need for external binder services.*

*   **Digital Project Binders:** Standardized folder structures (A-ritningar, K-ritningar, Administrativt, etc.).
*   **Version Control:** Automatically handle v1, v2 of drawings.
*   **External Sharing:** Secure links for subcontractors to access specific folders without full login.
*   **"Byggpärmen":** Digital implementation of the standardized Swedish construction binder.
*   **RFI (Request for Information):** Formalized Q&A flow linked to documents.
*   **Submittals:** Workflow for approving materials/documents from subs.

## 2. Safety & Compliance (The "SSG" Killer)
*Goal: Manage all safety and access control internally.*

*   **Certificate Tracking:** Database of employee certificates (Heta Arbeten, Lift, ID06) with expiry alerts.
*   **Site Induction (Arbetsplatsintroduktion):** Digital form/checklist that staff must sign before entering a project.
*   **Access Control:** Log who is on site (Personalliggare integration - check if we have this).
*   **Risk Analysis (Riskanalys):** Digital templates for daily risk assessments.
*   **Incident Reporting:** Mobile-friendly reporting of near-misses and accidents.

## 3. Drawings & Markups (The "Bluebeam" Lite)
*Goal: View and annotate drawings without leaving the app.*

*   **Built-in PDF Viewer:** Fast rendering of heavy construction drawings.
*   **Basic Markups:** Cloud, Arrow, Text, Highlight.
*   **Issue Tracking:** Pin a "Work Order" or "Deviation" directly onto a drawing location.
*   **Measurement Tools:** Simple scale calibration and area/length measurement.
*   **Overlay/Compare:** View two versions of a drawing on top of each other to see changes.

## 4. Calculation & Finance (The "Meps/Timekeep" Enhancer)
*Goal: Streamline the money flow.*

*   **Estimation Templates:** "Recipes" for common jobs (e.g., "Bathroom 5m2") to speed up quoting.
*   **ÄTA Management:** Tight integration between "Extra Work" and Invoicing.
*   **Timekeeping:** GPS Geofencing for automatic time tracking? Kiosk mode for site tablets?
*   **Budget vs Actual:** Real-time tracking of project costs against the quote.

## 5. Resource & Asset Management (The "Excel" Killer)
*Goal: Get rid of the whiteboards and spreadsheets.*

*   **Staff Scheduling:** Gantt chart or Calendar view of who is working where.
*   **Tool Tracking:** Inventory system for expensive tools (Hilti drills, lasers) - check in/out.
*   **Vehicle Log:** Mileage tracking and service reminders for company cars.

## 6. Communication (The "WhatsApp" Killer)
*Goal: Keep professional comms out of private apps.*

*   **Project Chat:** Dedicated channels for each project.
*   **Push Notifications:** Urgent alerts for site closures or safety issues.
*   **Daily Log (Dagbok):** Automated prompts for site supervisors to fill in the daily log.
